### Features

- **Neurable MW75 Neuro headphone support**: Added full session support for the Master & Dynamic MW75 Neuro EEG headphones (12 channels at 500 Hz). The `mw75` crate (with `rfcomm` feature) is added to `skill-devices` and re-exported. The MW75 uses a two-phase connection: BLE for activation (enable EEG + raw mode, query battery), then RFCOMM channel 25 for data streaming — the headphones must first be paired via OS Bluetooth settings (hold power button 4+ seconds to enter pairing mode). BLE scanner recognises MW75 devices by name and by MW75 GATT service UUID (required on macOS for already-paired Classic BT devices). Every ~15 seconds the scanner performs a targeted service-UUID scan to discover MW75 headphones that don't advertise a name. DSP pipeline processes the first 4 of 12 channels; all channels are written to CSV. Battery monitoring, DND focus-mode automation, and band snapshot enrichment fully integrated. MW75 constants (`MW75_EEG_CHANNELS`, `MW75_SAMPLE_RATE`, `MW75_CHANNEL_NAMES`) added to `skill-constants`.
